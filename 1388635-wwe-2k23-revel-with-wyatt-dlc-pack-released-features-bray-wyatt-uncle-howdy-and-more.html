WWE 2K23 Revel With Wyatt DLC Pack Released; Features Bray Wyatt, Uncle Howdy, And More

Bray Wyatt has finally arrived in WWE 2K23.

It was announced this morning in a press release from 2K that new stars from RAW, SmackDown, and NXT are coming to the game via the “Revel with Wyatt” DLC pack today to help fill out the game’s ever-growing roster.

The Revel with Wyatt pack includes:

  • Bray Wyatt
  • Uncle Howdy
  • Zeus
  • Valhalla
  • Joe Gacy
  • Blair Davenport

With the release of the Revel with Wyatt pack, only one more DLC pack remains for WWE 2K23.

  • Eve Torres
  • Wade Barrett
  • Damon Kemp
  • Andre Chase
  • Nathan Frazer

WWE 2K23 is currently available on the following platforms: PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Xbox Series X and Series S, and Microsoft Windows.

Bray Wyatt has been absent from WWE television for several months. He last appeared on the February 24 episode of WWE SmackDown and subsequent reports noted that he was dealing with a physical issue.

Despite his absence, Wyatt (and Bo Dallas) are both still with the company. Due to his absence, Uncle Howdy’s creative has also been on hold, but they are both still with WWE.
