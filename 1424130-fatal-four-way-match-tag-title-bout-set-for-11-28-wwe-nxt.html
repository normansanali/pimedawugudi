Fatal Four-Way Match, Tag Title Bout Set For 11/28 WWE NXT

Two big matches have been announced for next week’s WWE NXT.

Following Wes Lee’s recent return to action, Dominik Mysterio has made him a deal — Lee can have another shot at the NXT North American Championship if he wins a four-way against three other former champions. Lee’s opponents include Cameron Grimes, Bronson Reed, and Johnny Gargano.

Meanwhile, two families will collide for the NXT Tag Team Championship as Tony D’Angelo and Channing “Stacks” Lorenzo will defend against Angel Garza and Humberto Carrillo. The latter team was victorious over Edris Enofe and Malik Blade on November 21, while D’Angelo and Stacks celebrated recently regaining their gold.

Next week’s episode will also include a pair of Iron Survivor Challenge qualifying matches selected by WWE Hall of Famer Jerry “The King” Lawler, while Andre Chase will break his silence over recent events surrounding Chase U.

Below is the updated lineup for November 28:

  • : Tony D’Angelo & Channing “Stacks” Lorenzo vs. Angel Garzo & Humberto Carrillo
  • : Wes Lee vs. Cameron Grimes vs. Bronson Reed vs. Johnny Gargano
  • Jerry Lawler selects the next Iron Survivor Challenge qualifying matches
  • Andre Chase breaks his silence

WrestleZone will have complete coverage of NXT as it airs next week.
