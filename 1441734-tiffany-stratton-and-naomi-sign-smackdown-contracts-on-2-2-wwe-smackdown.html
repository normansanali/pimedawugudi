Tiffany Stratton And Naomi Sign SmackDown Contracts On 2/2 WWE SmackDown

WWE SmackDown has added two stars to the women’s division.

On the February 2 episode of WWE SmackDown, Naomi stepped out of Nick Aldis’ holding a SmackDown contract, and it was clear that she had signed with the blue brand. Moments later, Tiffany Stratton arrived and revealed that she also signed a SmackDown contract.

Stratton called herself the “center of the universe” and said she was not afraid to step up to the baddest woman in WWE, as she got into Bianca Belair’s face. She then rocked Michin, who was standing nearby, with a cheap shot.

Naomi made her return to WWE at WWE Royal Rumble, as she competed in the Women’s Royal Rumble. She previously walked out of WWE in May 2022. During her time away from the company, she had a successful run in TNA, as she won the Knockouts World Championship.

Stratton is a former NXT Women’s Champion. She also competed in the 2024 Women’s Royal Rumble.
