Mabel Jane Grace Is Not an Only Child Yet Her Sibling's Identity Is Hidden

Mabel Jane Grace is the first child of Topher Grace and Ashley Hinshaw. She has a younger sibling and should soon be the eldest of three. Her siblings remain a mystery.

Mabel Jane Grace was born on November 1, 2017. Her parents are well-accomplished actors who have worked with some of Hollywood's best directors and producers. Her father, Topher Grace, is well known for playing Eric Forman in "That '70s Show."

Her mother is an actress more recently recognized for her role in Crackle's TV series, "StartUp." Mabel's parents are very private people who have concealed their children's identities.

Mabel Jane Grace Gave Her Dad the Gift of Fatherhood

A simple day out, grabbing something to eat, turned into a paparazzi fest for Topher and Ashley. The "About Cherry" star was seen in a blue and white striped dress with a tight fit that exposed the silhouette of her baby bump. Her husband took it easy in a pair of denim and a blue T-shirt.

They prefer keeping their personal experiences private, but there was no control over breaking such news that day. They were just over a year into marriage at the time of this discovery. Without any children, this pregnancy was a new experience for both of them, although Topher is the godfather of Jaime King's son.

The news had already hit the media when Ashley took to Instagram to make the announcement herself. She posted a photo of herself with a caption that revealed she had been pregnant for six and a half months. They managed to conceal it for most of the pregnancy.

Ashley Hinshaw and Topher Grace at the 25th Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards at The Shrine Auditorium, Los Angeles, CA, on January 27, 2019. | Source: Getty Images

Ashley hosted a baby shower and shared pictures of the event on Instagram on October 22. She credited Vanessa Felice for putting the event together and entertaining her guests.

On November 1, 2017, Topher and Ashley welcomed their baby girl, Mabel Jane Grace. Ashley was 28 years old then, and her husband of a year and a half was 39.

Mabel Jane Grace's Birth Helped Her Dad Find Purpose in His Role in "BlacKkKlansman"

Mabel was born when her father was preparing for a Spike Lee film to create awareness of the racial ills that have preceded the world as we know it. Topher suffered a lot while preparing for his role as the Klu Klux Klan grand Wizard, David Duke, who, upon researching, invoked feelings of depression.

The actor struggled with learning his lines from the film with a plot based in 1970 Colorado. With such a sensitive theme in the movie, Mabel's father had to memorize some truly hateful words, much to his wife's displeasure. He added:

"I'd be rehearsing lines, and [my wife] would be like, 'Hey, can you like chill out with the hate speech right now? Our daughter's two weeks old.'"

Luckily for Topher, he was a father to a baby girl that brought him a lot of joy. He feels that actors are just playing pretend for a living, but with a topic like this and the birth of his little one, he found purpose in shooting this film and found it to be a cathartic experience.

Mabel Jane Grace Lives in Obscurity, but Her Sibling's Name Is Also a Mystery

Mabel's parents have managed to keep her private and out of the public eye. However, in 2020, she became an older sibling to a child who remains a complete mystery to the public. Mabel's parents have not disclosed any details about her younger sibling; not even the child's gender.

When she was pregnant with her second child, Ashley spoke about her excitement of becoming a mother of two, and months into her pregnancy showed off her beautiful baby bump. A misleading build-up for fans who anticipated a reveal.

When Ashley was in the hospital recovery room after their second child, she felt it was okay for Topher to leave as it was not her first experience. However, he stuck around until she recovered. The "That 70's Show" star mentioned that he was excited about baby number two and looking forward to getting even less sleep.

In 2022, Ashley is pregnant with the third edition of their family, Topher shared on Kelly Clarkson's "The Kelly Clarkson Show." While on the show, Topher saw that announcement as the perfect opportunity to send his appreciation to his wife; he added:

"And I'd like to take this moment on national TV to say how beautiful and amazing she is."

Ashley is against the oppression of women and their right to plan their pregnancies. She shared a post on Instagram expressing her support for women's rights. A portion of the caption details that she's been on the board of a foster care organization for the past three years.

Although the couple lives a better life, they do not keep their children a secret. It is public knowledge that they are parents of two, and the third is on the way. Ashley even expands on her history, detailing that she has been pregnant five times before and only given birth twice.

The sensitive details that she expresses are very personal to her, and she drives that energy into her activism for other women's birth rights. With their family blossoming and careers peaking simultaneously, there is much to expect from the Graces.
