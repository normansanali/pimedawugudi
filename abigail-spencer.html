Abigail Spencer Movies and TV Shows

Abigail Leigh Spencer (born August 4, 1981) is an American actress. She gained recognition for her role as Rebecca Tyree on the ABC soap opera All My Children (1999–2001). Spencer has had recurring roles on popular television shows like Mad Men (2009), Hawthorne (2009–2011), Suits (2011–2019), and Grey's Anatomy (2017–2022). She received critical acclaim for her portrayal of Amantha Holden in the SundanceTV drama series Rectify (2013–2016). Spencer also starred as history professor Lucy Preston in the NBC series Timeless (2016–2018). In addition to her television work, she has appeared in various films, including Cowboys & Aliens (2011) and Oz the Great and Powerful (2013).
