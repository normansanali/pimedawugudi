Amal Clooney Gives Birth to Twins Amal and George Clooney Welcome Twins

George and Amal Clooney's twins are finally here! The couple welcomed their first children on Tuesday—a girl and a boy named Ella and Alexander.

A rep from the couple confirmed the news to The Hollywood Reporter: "Ella, Alexander and Amal are all healthy, happy and doing fine. George is sedated and should recover in a few days."

It was first announced in February that the actor and human rights attorney were expecting twins. Congratulations to the happy couple!
