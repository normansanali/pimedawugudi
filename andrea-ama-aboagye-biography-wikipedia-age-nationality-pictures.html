Andrea Ama Aboagye Biography: Wikipedia, Age, Nationality, Pictures

Andrea Ama Aboagye is a British-Ghanaian actress known for her role as Sade in the sitcom “Meet the Adebanjos” alongside Wale Ojo.

Ama Aboagye is also known for Murder Capital , Venus vs Mars and so on. She was born in November 24.
