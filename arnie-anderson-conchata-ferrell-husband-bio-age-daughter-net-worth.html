Arnie Anderson Biography

Arnie Anderson is an American sound mixer and the husband of the late American actress Conchata Ferrell who was best known for her role as Berta in Two and a Half Men. She died on October 12, 2020 at the age of 77 due to complications following a cardiac arrest.

Arnie Anderson Age

His age is unknown but his wife was 77 years old at the time of his death.

Arnie Anderson Career

Arnie is best known for appearing in the 1975 documentary The California Reich, directed by Keith Critchlow and Walter E. Parkes. The documentary received a Best Documentary Academy Award nomination in 1976, for its depiction of Los Angeles, San Francisco, and Tracy, California Neo-Nazi groups. The same year it was screened at the Cannes Film Festival.

Arnie Anderson Wife

Anderson married his wife Conchata Ferrell in the mid 1980’s. Conchata was an award-winning stage actress in the 1970s, Ferrell was a frequent TV guest star throughout the ’80s and ’90s on shows like Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Friends, Who’s the Boss? and Night Court. She earned an Emmy nomination for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series in 1992 for her role as entertainment lawyer Susan Bloom on NBC’s L.A. Law.

She was best known for her role as housekeeper Berta on CBS’ Two and a Half Men; it was a recurring role at first, but Ferrell was promoted to series regular in Season 2 and remained with the series for its entire 12-season run. Her final TV role was in five episodes of Netflix’s The Ranch in 2017.

Arnie Anderson Daughter

Arnie and his wife have a daughter known as Samantha Anderson.

Conchata Ferrell Illness

In May 2020 Ferrell was hospitalized with a kidney related infection he suffered in December. She spent a month in intensive care, during which she suffered a heart attack. She was on a respirator and dialysis, and was unable to speak or communicate.

According to TMZ, Arnie said; “It’s going to be a while before there’s any recovery. It’s all neurological. There’s nothing we can do to speed it up the process. We are hoping for the best.”

Conchata Ferrell Death

Conchata died on October 12, 2020 at the age of 77 as a result of complications following a cardiac arrest. She died peacefully surrounded by family at Sherman Oaks Hospital in Sherman Oaks, California according to Deadline.
