Aubrey ODay: Pauly Ds Pierced Penis Triggers the Right Spots

Um, TMI. Aubrey O’Day opens up about her sex life with boyfriend Paul “DJ Pauly D” DelVecchio — and his pierced penis — in a new sneak peek from the Tuesday, August 2, episode of Famously Single.

During a night out with gal pals Brandi Glanville, Jessica White and Somaya Reece, the former Danity Kane singer, 32, dishes about a wild night in the bedroom with the Jersey Shore alum, 36, and his, ahem, manhood.

“We’ve been having really good sex,” O’Day tells her costars. “We’ve had a good amount of sex in the past [day].”

Things then take a NSFW turn as the reality star reveals, “He has his, um, penis pierced.”

After Glanville’s jaw drops and Reece’s eyes widen, White asks O’Day if she likes her beau’s risqué body piercing.

“It triggers the right spots,” the model divulges.

Seconds later, Real Housewives of Beverly Hills alum Glanville, 43, tells the cameras, “Aubrey is an oversharer. Pauly, I mean, I’m sorry.”

This isn’t the first time O’Day has addressed sex with Pauly D on Famously Single. During the July 19 episode, she couldn’t help but think about taking him to the bedroom.

“How long is a good time to wait before you have sex with someone?” the blonde bombshell asked her pals at the time. “If you’re seeing them every day, like, let’s say in this type of situation, how long would you wait?”
