Bea Alonzo - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Facts, Single

Table of Biography

Bea Alonzo is a famous Filipino actress, model, social media star, and singer. Bea Alonzo is famous for her work in ‘One More Chance (2007)’, ‘The Love Affair (2015)’, and ‘I Heart Betty La Fea’ (2008).

Early Life

Bea Alonzo was born Phylbert Angelli Ranollo Fagerstrom on October 17, 1987. She was born in Cainta, Rizal, Philippines. Presently, she is 36 years old and her zodiac sign is Libra.

Moreover, her father is British, and her mother, Mary Anne Ranollo Filipino. Sadly, her parents separated when she was just 4 years old.

Further, she is the eldest of four siblings. Likewise, in 2001, she finished elementary education at the Jose Abad Santos Memorial School in Quezon City. Later, she joined Fisher Valley College in Taguig.

And, she also attended Ususan Elementary School in Ususan. After that, she joined Colegio de Santa Ana in Taguig.

Career

Bea was discovered in a local town beauty contest and she was later referred to audition at ABS-CBN. And, after so many auditions and go-sees, finally, she was asked to undergo a workshop.

However, in the year 2002, Bea earned recognition in the world for the TV Primetime Soap Opera “Kay Tagal Kang Hinintay”. And, in 2003, she proceeded with ‘It Might Be You’ a follow-up project.

In 2005, ‘Ikaw Ang Lahat Sa Akin’ starred alongside actors Claudine Barretto Angelika Dela Cruz, and Diether Ocampo. Likewise, in 2006, more recognition poured in for the Soap Opera Maging Sino Ka Man and its second book Maging Sino Ka Man: Ang Pagbabalik.

Similarly, in the year 2007, her film ‘One More became a cult success at the box office a sequel released in 2015 gave more appreciation. In 2014, she starred in ‘The Love Affair’.

Then, in 2018, she did 2 more films the horror suspense film ‘First Love’ and ‘Kasal.’ Moreover, as of 2021, she is exclusively signed with GMA Network.

More detail

She released her debut album The Real Me under Star Records in June 2008. Also in mid-2008, it was disclosed that she would play the leading character Betty Pengson in the TV sitcom ‘Yo Soy Betty La Fea’, which originated in Colombia.

Therefore, the Philippines’ version was entitled ‘I Love Betty La Fea.’

Further, the show premiered in September 2008 at the primetime slot. And, it ran for eight consecutive months. Likewise, in December 2011, Bea renewed her contract with ABS-CBN. Moreover, she did two movies under Star Cinema and a reunion drama ‘The Mistress’.

Bea appeared in the 2014 international hit TV drama ‘Sana Bukas pa ang Kahapon (Tomorrow Belongs To Me)’. And, in the year 2019, she appeared in the horror film ‘Eerie’ and the dramatic film ‘Unbreakable’.

Also, she began pursuing writing for film in 2019 by enrolling in the writing workshop of Filipino screenwriter, journalist, novelist, and playwright, Ricky Lee.

Likewise, in the year 2021, she was included in the list of Variety.com and the International Film Festival & Awards Macao as among Asia’s biggest stars poised for international stardom.

Furthermore, it was announced by GMA News that Bea transferred to GMA Network after almost 20 years under ABS-CBN Entertainment management on July 1, 2021.
