Channing Tatum Is the Prettiest Unicorn of All Time

This weekend, Channing Tatum proved, once and for all, that he is the most impressive unicorn in all the land. As if there was ever any doubt. He accessorized the outfit by carrying a festive pumpkin, and wearing a rainbow tail. Needless to say, he looked flawless.
