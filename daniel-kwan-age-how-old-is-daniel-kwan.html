Daniel Kwan Age: How old is Daniel Kwan?

Daniel Kwan and his colleague, Daniel Scheinert, are called the Daniels. These are film directors, producers, and screenwriters from the United States.

They began their career as music video filmmakers, including the well-known DJ Snake and Lil Jon music video for the tune “Turn Down for What” (2013).

Since then, they’ve written and directed the bizarre black comedy-drama Swiss Army Man (2016) and the science-fiction picture Everything Everywhere All at Once (2022).

The latter became A24’s highest-grossing film of all time, and the Duo received Academy Award nominations for Best Picture, Best Director, and Best Original Screenplay.

Daniel Kwan Age: How old is Daniel Kwan?

Daniel Kwan, one-half of the directing pair Daniels, was born on February 17, 1988. He is 35 years old as of March 2023.

As of 2022, the Daniels have signed a first-look TV deal with A24. The same year, they also signed a five-year film deal with Universal Pictures.

The Daniels were on the Time 100 Next list in 2022.
