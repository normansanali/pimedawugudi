Dua Lipa Pairs a Gauzy Ivory Dress with Matching Sheer Pantaboots

Dua Lipa upped the ante with her latest monochromatic outfit by relying on some standout sheer pieces, courtesy of Bottega Veneta.

The “Dance the Night” singer shared a series of new outfit photos on Instagram today, in which she posed in an all-white look while standing on the sandy banks of a pond. The outfit consisted of a completely see-through midi dress, which showed off white underwear and a bra underneath. The gauzy dress additionally featured a draping cowl neckline, plus strips of silver buttons down the sides of the torso and a self-tie detail at the back.

To coordinate, Lipa wore matching sheer ivory pantaboots, whose skintight silhouette reached all the way up to her mid-thigh. The unique footwear included geometric lining down the front and on the feet, as well as towering clear PVC heels. Finally, Lipa finished off the look with silver twisted corkscrew earrings, which also hailed from Bottega Veneta.

“Yesterday I found out i’m actually a gemini rising,” she captioned the pictures. “Someone unpack that for me.”

The Grammy winner's affinity for sheer fashion statements is well documented on her Instagram account.

She previously posted another sheer monochromatic look earlier this month. At the time, she opted for an icy blue long-sleeved mesh minidress with a matching neck scarf from Aya Muse. Underneath, she repped a bedazzled baby blue bikini from Alessandra Rich. To elevate the ensemble, Lipa added a pink-and-white plaid box purse from Chanel, metallic platform pumps from her collaboration with Versace, oval Cartier sunglasses, and plenty of gold jewelry.
