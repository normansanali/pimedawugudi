This new skin cleanser doubles up as a face mask - and youll love it

It's a multitasking dream...

    New beauty brand Faace may have only launched last year, but is now a firm favourite with skincare lovers across the globe - and we can see why. Made in the UK, and fully vegan, the brand has just dropped two new facial cleansers - Dull Face and Dirty Face - taking a renewed approach to skincare that’s totally fuss free. Interested? Read on...

    Dull Faace, £24, Faace

    SHOP NOW

    One of the new products, Dull Faace, doubles up as a cleanser-mask dup to help combat impurities and a lacklustre skin tone. Made with Kaolin clay (which is a great ingredient for oily skin types), green tea, turmeric, vitamin C and vitamin E, this skincare hero is packed full of antioxidants that fight free radicals.

    Meanwhile, the citric acid (AHA) provides gentle exfoliation, and the hyaluronic acid keeps skin moisture in check. Oooh! Use it daily as a simple cleanser, or leave it on for ten minutes as a mask to help draw out impurities from your pores. Job done.

    Dirty Faace, £23, Faace

    SHOP NOW

    You may be a gym lover wanting to remove all traces of sweat from your face, or a city dweller longing to rid your skin of pesky pollution particles. Whatever you are, if you feel like you have ‘dirty’ skin, this product has been designed specifically for you. 

    Formulated with coconut fatty acids, rose water, aloe extract, vitamin C and vitamin C, this cleanser offers a creamy lather without drying out your skin. And yes, it really will leave you feeling squeaky clean!
