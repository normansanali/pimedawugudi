Does Fallout 4 know how to say your name?

Watch on YouTube | Subscribe to Polygon on YouTube

Fallout 4 introduces a companion named Codsworth, a robot butler who, among other talents, can say a whole bunch of names. According to Bethesda, more than 1,000 names were read by voice actor Stephen Russell — but is your handle one of the lucky ones?

We tested nearly 100 names against Codsworth for several hours yesterday, and you can find some of the highlights in the video above. We can also just save you some time: If you're named after a famous sci-fi heroine, great news, you're in the game.
