Gucci Mane Wife: Meet Keyshia Ka’Oir: Gucci Mane, officially known as Radric Delantic Davis was born on February 12, 1980, in Bessemer, Alabama, United States to Ralph Everett Dudley and Vicky Jean Davis.

Gucci Mane was arrested after being caught in possession of crack cocaine by an undercover police officer and was sentenced to 90 days in county jail and then probation.
