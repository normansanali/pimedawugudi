How Did Wyatt Langford Get His Nickname &quot;Trenton Thunder&quot;?

From the tiny town of Trenton, Florida, to the center of the baseball world - that's the story of Wyatt Langford, the future of the MLB. Langford, who was born on November 15, 2001, took the spectacular route to becoming an outfielder for the Major League Baseball's Texas Rangers. How did he get his nickname, "Trenton Thunder"?


The Rise of "Trenton Thunder"

Nicknamed "Trenton Thunder" for the powerful home runs he hit and respect garnered at Trenton High School in Florida. The road for Langford began at Trenton High School where not only played baseball but also football and basketball.
