Morganna Roberts (born July 4, 1954) is an entertainer who was renowned in baseball and other sports as Morganna or Morganna, the Kissing Bandit from the 1970s through the 1990s. When she was a dancer in the 1980s, she was also known as “Morganna the Wild One.” Morganna famously stormed the field and kissed Major League Baseball players such as Nolan Ryan, Pete Rose, Johnny Bench, George Brett (twice), Steve Garvey, and Cal Ripken, Jr. on numerous occasions. She’s been dubbed “baseball’s unofficial mascot” and “baseball’s grand dame.” She also crashed NBA games, with Kareem Abdul-Jabbar being one of her most famous victims.
