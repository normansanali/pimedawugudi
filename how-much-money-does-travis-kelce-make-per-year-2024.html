Overview of Travis Kelce's Contract

Travis Kelce, the tight end for the Kansas City Chiefs, signed a four-year, $57.25 million contract extension in 2020. The contract included $27 million in guarantees, with $20.25 million guaranteed at signing. Kelce's base salaries for 2020 and 2021 were fully guaranteed, and there are per game roster bonuses in every year of the contract. Additionally, Kelce converted his 2021 roster bonus to a signing bonus for cap purposes, reducing his cap number by $5.6 million. Furthermore, the Chiefs moved $1 million of Kelce's 2023 base salary into 2022 and added $2 million in 53-man per game roster bonuses.

Cash Flows and Career Earnings

Kelce's career earnings stand at $78,266,231, with a potential total earnings of $108,516,231. His total guarantees amount to $32,220,304, with a largest cash payment of $17,250,000 in 2025.
