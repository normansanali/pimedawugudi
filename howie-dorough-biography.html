Howie Dorough | Biography

One of the most loved faces of the popular band the Backstreet Boys, Howie Dorough originally named Howard Dwaine Dorough is a prolific American musician, song-writer, entertainer, dancer, actor and entrepreneur. He rose to fame as a member of Backstreet Boys, and since has branched out into several other ventures leaving his mark on each of them. Florida’s native, Howie Dorough took birth during the year 1973 on 22 August. Born in Orlando to Paula Flores and Hoke Dorough, Howie is the youngest of 5 siblings. His heritage is part Latin from his mom side and part Irish from his dad side. He attended Edgewater High School and graduated in 1991.

Moving into the personal life of this hot singer, he has always been a generous and gentle man. Despite of being a hot favorite singer and dream date of many girls, his commitment towards his relationship is commendable. Sticking to one woman, he married his long term lady love Leigh Boniello Dorough on 8 December 2007 in a traditional catholic ritual. The couple had commenced dating in 2001 and announced their engagement in new year eve of 2006. The couple welcomed their first baby boy, James Hoke Dorough on 6 May 2009. On 16 February 2013, they gave birth to their second child named Holden John Dorough. After losing his sister, Caroline Dorough-Cochran to lupus, Howie founded the Dorough Lupus Foundation. The foundation works to raise awareness regarding the disease, money for research and provide financial assistance to help the victim of this disease whose economic condition is bad.

The heartthrob of music lovers from all around the world, Howie Dorough music career is the result of his childhood passion for the music. As a child he used to perform in the church choirs and school and act in some local theater groups. Stepped into acting with flicks like Cop and Half and parenthood, he was meant to be in the spotlight. Though started with small roles, much bigger things were brewing for this rising star. His audition for the BBs under the title Tony Donetti in 1992 opened his path to stardom. Selected as the band member of Backstreet Boys, Howie was all set to literally set fire in the music industry with his talent. After forming band in 1993, they started to perform in several venues including shopping malls and high schools and ultimately managed to bagged contract of Jive Records in following year. After building up huge European fan base, the Backstreet Boys put out their very first album stateside. The band launched “Backstreet’s Back”, their second album in Europe. The album was a big hit. “Millennium”, their third album hit the market in 1999. The album as well topped the chart. Over the next some year, the band racked up super hits. BBs’s other album include “This Is Us”, “Unbreakable”, “Never Gone” and “In A World Like This”. Among entire band member, Howie took the longest time to launch his solo singing career with his debut album “Back to Me”, released in 2011. His first single enjoyed moderate success. Spreading his wings in other ventures, he guested in the TV sitcom Sabrina, the Teenage Witch and on one of the episode of Roswell as well as voiced Santa Claus in the animated series called Dora the Explorer. He along with his band member starred in the “This Is the End” in 2013. Entering into the business world, he has funded a corporation named Sweet D, Inc. along with his brother John. He as well held the prestigious positions of director, chairman and CEO in multiple companies; including Howiedoit Productions, Inc. this American musician has garnered net worth of forty-five million dollars through his profession till date.

As of his profession and childhood passion, Howie loves sing and acting. He prefers to spend his precious time with his wife, Leigh Dorough and his children. With every passing time, the craze of Howie is growing among his fans. He has successfully established himself as an accomplished singer in American musical industry. With his beautiful voice and charismatic persona, he has been dominating heart of music lovers since his very first appearance. He has millions of fan following all over the world.
