Jenny Walser (Tori Spring Actress): Who played Tori Spring in Heartstopper?

Jennifer Catherine Walser is an English actress.

Jennifer Walser was born in 1995.

Jennifer Walser was born in London and she has a sister named Francesca.

Jennifer Walser attended the Rona Hart School of Dance and was a member of the RADA Youth Company as a child.

Jennifer Walser graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Natural Sciences from Durham University in 2016.

Jennifer Walser participated in theatre productions and joined National Youth Theatre REP Company.

Jennifer Walser stars as Tori Spring in the Netflix adaptation of Heartstopper, a British coming-of-age romance streaming television series developed for Netflix based on the webcomic and graphic novel of the same name by Alice Oseman.

The series was released on 22 April 2022 and has received critical acclaim.

See-Saw Films optioned the television rights of Heartstopper in July 2019 and on 20 January 2021, it was revealed that Netflix ordered an 8-half hour episode series, with Oseman herself as writer and Euros Lyn as director and executive producer.

Also executive producing are Patrick Walters, Jamie Laurenson, Hakan Kousetta, Iain Canning, and Emile Sherman with Zorana Piggott as producer.
