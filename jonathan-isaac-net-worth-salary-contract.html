Who Is Jonathan Isaac?

Jonathan Isaac was born on 3rd October 1997 in Naples, Florida to Jackie Allen (mother). He also has one older sister, two older brothers, and two younger brothers. As a teen, he attended Florida State University where he played college basketball for the Seminoles.

In 2017, Jonathan Isaac was drafted by the Orlando Magic as the sixth overall pick in the 2017 NBA Draft. He went ahead to make his NBA debut from the 2017 to 2018 season. Jonathan played in 27 games and averaged 5.4 points and 3.7 rebounds per game. Jonathan continued to improve his performance in the 2018 to 2019 season. He became a regular starter for the Magic and averaged 9.6 points and 5.5 rebounds per game. In the 2019 to 2020 season, he was named to the NBA All-Defensive Second Team and went ahead to play in the Rising Stars Challenge during the All-Star Weekend.
