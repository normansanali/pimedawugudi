The Flash Actress Kausar Mohammed Wiki | Bio

Kausar Mohammed (born on February 11, 1992) is a 29 years old American actress of Saudi origin. She is also a comedian and known for her performance in “ViacomCBS Sketch Comedy Showcase.” Her looks allow her to portray characters of different nationalities like Indian, Pakistani, Bangladeshi, and Arabian. One of the most beloved characters she played was Yaz/Yasmina Fadoula in a popular animated series titled “Jurassic World: Camp Cretaceous.”

In 2022, she climbed the ladder of success after landing the role of Dr. Meena Dhawan in the popular CW series “The Flash.” It was the first time that people got to see Kausar playing a prominent role.

Early Life

On February 11, 1992, Kausar Mohammed (age 29) was born to her mother Ezmina Mohammed, and father in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. There is not much data about Kausar’s father, but we managed to find a photograph of Mrs. Mohammed. The actress also has a sister named Sanaa Fidai, who is married and a nurse by profession.

Sources revealed that Kausar was a child when she moved with her family to San Jose, California, U.S. Here she completed her schooling at a local school and attended UCLA. The Arabian girl was in school when she decided that she would grow up to be an actress.

Career & Personal Life

During her time at UCLA, Kausar Mohammed discovered that besides acting, she possesses the talent to make people laugh, so she joined Lesly Kahn & Co. to develop her skills. Just like the actors, she connected with a talent manager and began auditioning. In 2013, Kausar started her career after landing the role of Pinky in a TV series titled “Pinky and Rupesh.” The show ran for one year; after that, she starred in a show named “Dirty Laundry.”

On the one hand, she excelled in the tv industry; on the other hand, she struggled to start in the American film industry. Despite her continuous efforts, all she got were chances to act in short movies like “Vetting,” “Homebody,” “Lights,” “The Launch Date,” “Courtyard,” and “Undergrads.”

From 2016 to 2018, she worked in 14 shows, some of which were “I Love Dick,” “Idiotsitter,” “I Want My Phone Back,” “Speechless,” “Unfair & Ugly,” “Nobodies,” “Smosh,” “East of La Brea” and “The Resident.” She was also part of a podcast named “Solve: The Podcast.”

In 2019 Kausar landed a role in Taraji P. Henson’s fantasy-comedy movie “What Men Want.” Here her role was Jenna Abbiddi, and with her splendid performance, she managed to impress all the viewers.

Currently, Kausar lives with her mom in San Jose, and if trusted sources are believed, her net worth is USD 598k.
