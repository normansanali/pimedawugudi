Fans Think Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny Had a New Year's Eve Rendezvous

Though Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny (real name: Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio) reportedly called it quits and went their separate ways before the winter holidays, fans believe the duo had a reunion before 2024 arrived. E! News reports that in a now-expired Instagram Story posted by Kendall's friend Renell Medrano, Bunny can be heard wishing everyone a "feliz Nuevo Año." Who was everyone, exactly? Kendall, Hailey and Justin Bieber, and their pals, who all closed out 2023 in Barbados together during what could have been the most epic group hang, ever.

Jenner and Bunny dated for less than a year before reports surfaced of a breakup on Dec. 17. Many believe the relationship fizzled out long before that since the two hadn't been seen together since October of 2023. Neither Jenner nor Bunny have officially said anything about the breakup.

Jenner's continued friendship (or more) with Bunny is in line with comments she made last year. In an interview, she explained that she loves "really hard" and hates saying goodbye. 

"I will fight to not have to say good-bye," the model told Harper's Bazaar. "I love really hard and I love without apology."

For his part, Bunny is unlikely to say anything about the relationship. In an interview, he said that he doesn't feel the need to explain his personal life to fans or anyone else — going as far as saying that he'd prefer if they didn't know anything about him at all. 

"They don't know how you feel, they don't know how you live, they don't know anything, and I really don't want them to know," he told Vanity Fair. "I'm not really interested in clarifying anything because I have no commitment to clarify anything to anyone."
