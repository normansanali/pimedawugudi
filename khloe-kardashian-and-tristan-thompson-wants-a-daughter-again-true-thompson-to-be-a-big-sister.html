Khloe Kardashian and Tristan Thompson wants a daughter again! True Thompson to be a big sister!

Updated On December 1, 2023 | Published By: Brandy Stratton | In Child , Pregnant

Khloe Kardashian is not over baby fever! Khloe who shares a 2 years old daughter True Thompson with Tristan Thompson wants more babies. During the recent trailer of Keeping Up with the Kardashian, Khloe talked about her decision to freeze her eggs. In a clip, Khloe is holding a syringe and said,

“You’re trusting a surrogate with your unborn child, and it’s just scary.”

On the show The Ellen Show, she shared that she has done IVF three different times and she has already frozen her eggs. Likewise, she wants to make embryos with Tristan so Khloe defrosted all of her 12-14 eggs to mix with sperm. Sadly none of her eggs survived, so she had to undergo IVF again to make embryos.

The youngest Kardashian daughter said,

“We realized that my eggs aren’t strong enough to be frozen. They should be mixed immediately with sperm to make embryos. So I actually have made embryos,”

More on the relationship

Not just that, Khloe shared about the difficulty of expanding family during the covid-19 pandemic. It became challenging for her to acquire assistance during the Covid. Khloe hopes to give her daughter True a sibling and said,

“My plan was to have kids closer in age. But now with COVID and everything, my plan’s been a little delayed. I definitely do want more kids. I have so many brothers and sisters. Also, I think it’s such a blessing—especially during these times—to have a family member or people that you can play with and rely on and just have a buddy through life.”

Khloe always had fear of getting pregnant as her parents had shown her photos which scared her. When she started using birth controls, she felt safer. She did not want to be a teen mom.

We hope to see more Kar-Jen babies in the future as Kendall Jenner also revealed her want to become a mother.
