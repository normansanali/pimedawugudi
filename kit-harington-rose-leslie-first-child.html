Kit Harington and Rose Leslie Welcome First Child

Game of Thrones couple Kit Harington and Rose Leslie have welcomed their first child, a baby boy, together. The new parents are "very very happy" about their new addition, Harington's rep confirmed to E! News. We still don't know the baby's name, however.

The actor duo were seen stepping out in London with their bundle of joy today. Dressed casually in jeans and winter coats, Harington and Leslie held hands, while the new mom carried their newborn in a baby carrier. The actress, who married her costar in 2018, announced her pregnancy in September with a photo shoot in Make magazine. She also told The New York Post the following month, "I am thrilled to be expecting, and I can't wait to meet the new member of our family!"

The couple met in 2012 portraying on-screen love interests Ygritte and Jon Snow in HBO's wildly popular fantasy series. Though they kept their relationship fairly out of the spotlight, they announced their engagement (in the paper) in 2017. Their dreamy summer wedding in Aberdeenshire, Scotland, saw a number of their costars in attendance, including Sophie Turner, Emilia Clarke, and Peter Dinklage. "I met my wife in this show, so in that way it gave me my future family and my life from here on in," Harington has said of meeting Leslie on Game of Thrones.

While opening up to Make magazine, Leslie revealed that she and her husband recently bought a Tudor manor in East Anglia. She calls the home "the house that Jon Snow built." The actress will appear in the film adaptation of Agatha Christie's Death on the Nile, costarring with Gal Gadot, Letitia Wright, Kenneth Branagh, and more, later this year.
