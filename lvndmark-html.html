LVNDMARK - Age, Family, Bio

Gamer who posts simulation, shooter, and RPG gameplay on his Lvndmark Twitch channel. He has accumulated over 2 million hours of watch time from his audience of 1 million followers. He also has a merch store where fans can purchase shirts, hats and shorts designed with his logo and other channel-related decorations.

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel in 2019, posting gaming clips and highlights. 

Trivia

He is a weaponry enthusiast, featuring guns like the SA58 on his Instagram channel and footage at the shooting range. He was selected as a Twitch partner by G Fuel, the energy drink brand. He has amassed over 460,000 subscribers and 235 million views.

Family Life

He was born and raised in the United States.

Associated With

He was interviewed for the YouTube channel PKA Clips by gamer and host Kyle Myers.
