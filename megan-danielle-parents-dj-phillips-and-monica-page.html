American Idol: Who Are Megan Danielle Parents?

Megan Danielle, the star of American Idol, is the daughter of DJ Phillips and Monica Page. She has shared about her family in the show. She disclosed her parents’ divorce when she was 7 years old before her top 20 performance. “My siblings and I actually watched my dad walking out of the house,” she said. “I put a lot of blame on myself for my dad leaving. It made me feel kind of worthless like I wasn’t a good daughter or even a good sister.”

Megan admitted that it’s been a “lifelong struggle” for her to believe in herself.

DJ and Monica welcomed Megan in 2002, on 10 February. She is reportedly working as a waitress.

Before American Idol, she had competed on The Voice. Megan participated in The Voice’s 18th season as a competitor. She belonged to Kelly Clarkson’s group. Before she was eliminated, she placed in the top 9. Michael Williams, another top 20 contender from the same season, competed against Megan as well.

Meet Monica Page, Megan Danielle Mother

Megan Danielle’s mother is named Monica Page.

On Monica’s birthday in June 2022, Megan wished her mom, “Happy Birthday to the most beautiful woman, my mama! ￼ I’m so thankful for you & all your hard work, raising us and being there for us through everything. I hope your day is amazing, 50-year-old Beauty Queen.”

Furthermore, on Mother’s Day, she wished her mom, “Happy Mother’s Day to the one I love the most. All my life I’ve had this woman to lean on and that has been the biggest blessing of all. She’s taught me how to love, how to take judgment, and how to be the bigger person when I don’t want to be. She is the strongest, most genuine person I know and she deserves so much. Love you, mama. 💜”

Monica is the daughter of Charles “Chuck” Thomas Page and Darlene Page. Her parents were married for 44 years. Her dad was born on February 4, 1957, the son of the late Junior Miller and the late Bertha Miller. Chuck was the owner and operator of  T and T Trucking for over 30 years. He was a member of Little Vine Baptist Church. He raced for 44 years. It was his favorite sport. He loved to watch his sons, Mark and Michael Page race.

Monica’s dad or Megan’s papa died on 25 November 2021.
