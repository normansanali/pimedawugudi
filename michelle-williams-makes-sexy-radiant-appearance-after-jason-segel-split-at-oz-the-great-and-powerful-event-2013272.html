Michelle Williams Makes Sexy, Radiant Appearance After Jason Segel Split at Oz The Great and Powerfu

Michelle Williams isn’t letting her romantic turmoil get in the way of work. Hours after Us Weekly exclusively broke news of her breakup with  Jason Segel, the 32-year-old actress was back in front of the cameras in Moscow, Russia to continue promotions for Oz The Great and Powerful.

The consummate professional fielded questions about the big-budget Wizard of Oz prequel during a Q&A panel, and then happily posed for photos with costars Mila Kunis and James Franco. The Oscar-nominated starlet giggled in one snapshot — showing off her lengthening blonde locks and her sexy, toned legs in a black tiered minidress and star-print heels. Williams plays Glinda, the Good Witch of the North in the epic fantasy based on L. Frank Baum’s Oz books.

As for her one-year romance with How I Met Your Mother‘s Segel, 33, “the breakup was mutual,” an insider tells Us Weekly. “It’s a sad moment. They respect each other.” Although the twosome made a valiant effort to make things work — Segel bonded with Matilda, Williams’ 7-year-old daughter with late ex Heath Ledger — their bicoastal romance proved too difficult to maintain, another inside explained.
