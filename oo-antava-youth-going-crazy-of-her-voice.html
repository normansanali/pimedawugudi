Oo Antava.. Youth Going Crazy Of Her Voice

Team Pushpa released the Sizzling song of the year yesterday. Oo Antava Oo Oo Antava is the song from the movie that was released on Youtube yesterday. Featuring Samantha in this special item number, the song was crooned by Indravathi Chouhan. Her voice added a lot of beauty to the song and people are curious about the singer.

Indravathi Chouhan is one of the noted folk singers in Telugu and she shot to fame with this interesting number from Pushpa. Also, Indravathi is popular singer Mangli’s sister. Mangli’s original name is Satyavathi. Both Satyavathi and Indravathi started singing at a young age.

Indravathi Chouhan crooned a lot of folk numbers that earned a lot of views and likes on Youtube. She was also a participant in Bol Baby Bol singing reality show. Indravathi also sang Jajimogulali from the film George Reddy.

Oo Antava Oo Oo Antava was released yesterday and earned more than 1 Crore views already. With over 6 lakh likes, the song is currently in the second position on the trending charts.

Directed by Sukumar, the film’s audio album is composed by Devi Sri Prasad. Mythri Movie Makers is bankrolling the project.
