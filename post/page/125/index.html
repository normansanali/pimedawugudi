Is baking soda the same as baking powder?

Jul 29, 2024 The bottom line While both products appear similar, they're certainly not the same. Baking soda is sodium bicarbonate, which requires an acid and a liquid to become activated and help baked goods rise. Conversely, baking powder includes sodium bicarbonate, as well as an acid. It only needs a liquid to become activated. Can I use baking soda in place of baking powder? Keep in mind that baking soda has three times the power of baking powder, so 1/3 teaspoon of baking soda is equivalent to 1 teaspoon of baking powder.
