How the Royal Family Honored Prince William on His 37th Birthday

  • Prince William is celebrating his 37th birthday today (June 21).
  • Members of the royal family have dedicated kind messages to the Duke of Cambridge in honor of the occasion.
  • Prince Harry and Meghan Markle left William a short and sweet comment on Instagram.

Prince William turns 37 today, and the royal family has wasted no time in sharing well-wishes for him on social media. The royals usually post loving messages for birthday celebrants in the family, and there was no exception for the second in line for the throne.

Clarence House, the official residence of William's father Prince Charles and his wife, the Duchess of Cornwall, went the nostalgic route with a vintage father-son photo. The image, posted to Instagram, shows a young William in overalls and tiny buckled shoes being pushed in a swing by his dad.

The @RoyalFamily account, the official handle for the queen and other senior members of the family, shared a slideshow of Prince William throughout his life, including a few sweet moments with Her Majesty.
