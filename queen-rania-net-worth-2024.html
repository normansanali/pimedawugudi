Queen Rania’s Early Life and Career

Before delving into her net worth, it’s important to understand Queen Rania’s background. Born Rania Al-Yassin in Kuwait to Palestinian parents, she later moved to Jordan for work. After marrying then-Prince Abdullah in 1993, her life took a royal turn. Her career, however, began in the corporate world, which contributed to her personal wealth.

Education and Early Work

Rania’s education and early career were focused on business. She earned a degree in Business Administration from the American University in Cairo and worked for companies like Citibank and Apple Inc. in Amman, Jordan. This business acumen has served her well in managing her finances and investments.
