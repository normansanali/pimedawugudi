About

Notorious media mogul who built a global empire of newspapers, TV, and movies through holding company News Corporation.

Before Fame

He worked as the Editor of the Daily Express newspaper. By 1953, he was managing News Limited, which he inherited from his father.

Trivia

He resigned as News International Director in 2012, following the phone-hacking scandal that brought his Business practices under U.S. and U.K. scrutiny.

Family Life

He was born to Elisabeth and Keith Murdoch. He was married twice before wedding Wendi Deng in 1999. In 2013 he filed for divorce from Deng. He then married Jerry Hall in 2016. He has a total of six children: James, Lachlan, Elisabeth, Purdence, Chloe and Grace.

Associated With

He formed a close alliance with conservative prime minister Margaret Thatcher in the 1980s.
