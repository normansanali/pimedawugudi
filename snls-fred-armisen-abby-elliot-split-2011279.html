SNL's Fred Armisen, Abby Elliott Split

This funny romance is done.

Saturday Night Live costars Fred Armisen and Abby Elliott have called it quits on their real-life romance, an insider tells Us Weekly exclusively.

Armisen, 44, hooked up with bubbly Elliott, 24, soon after his June 2010 separation from wife Elisabeth Moss.

"Fred sought comfort in her after the split," a pal told Us last year. (Mad Men actress Moss, 29, filed for divorce in September 2010.)
