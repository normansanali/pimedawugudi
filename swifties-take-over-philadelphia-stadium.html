Swifties Take Over Philadelphia Stadium for Taylor Swift Shows

During Taylor Swift’s Philadelphia shows, thousands of fans gathered outside the stadium. The bizarre sight outside Philadelphia’s Lincoln Financial Field this past weekend was caused by the high demand for tickets to Taylor Swift’s “Eras Tour,” which would have required her to conduct more than 900 stadium shows.

Three of Swift’s shows for the weekend will take place at the stadium, which has an estimated concert capacity of slightly under 70,000. Despite this, thousands of Swifties still showed up to her gigs and watched from outside the stadium, where they sang and danced along for the entire three hours.

One Twitter user commented on the scene outside the Linc, writing, “Beatlesmania reincarnated as Swiftmania.” “If all the Swifties ever mobilized they could overthrow the whole country,” another joked. You may watch the video for yourself down below.

Swift’s “The Eras Tour” will play Gillette Stadium in Foxborough, Massachusetts for three nights after the final show in Philadelphia on Sunday night. You may get tickets to any of her forthcoming shows here.
