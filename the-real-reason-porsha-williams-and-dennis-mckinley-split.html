The Real Reason Porsha Williams And Dennis McKinley Split

Some fans may have been concerned that Porsha Williams was jumping into a relationship with Dennis McKinley too quickly, but come to find out, the pace might not have been entirely her fault. During an episode of The Real Housewives of Atlanta (via Radar Online), co-stars Kandi Burruss, Carmon Cambrice, and Jami Ziegler were gossiping about McKinley and claimed to have uncovered his modus operandi. "He falls pretty quickly — and he tattoos people's names on him," Burruss said. She then proceeded to dish about a woman she knew, claiming McKinley tattooed "her face and her name" on his body. Burruss also alleged that McKinley splurged on a Rolex watch for the woman in question. Ziegler also claimed that McKinley had her friend's name inked on him, too.

The televised exchange was dramatic, particularly considering the scandalous conversation was cut with scenes of a none-the-wiser Williams gushing to her mom about McKinley buying her a Rolex watch and showing her mom a picture of her name tattooed on McKinley's thigh. "He's gonna put my face on his back! This is for real, like, he ain't going nowhere," Williams swooned. Ouch.
