Tone Bell Net Worth

Tone Bell:

$2 million

Tone Bell is an American actor as well as a stand-up comedian. He had his career beginnings in the 2010s, debuting as Parker in 2 episodes of the TV series Single Ladies. He next became known for his role as RJ in the sitcom Whitney. In 2013, he made his film debut in Implanted. He later landed the role of Tedward Mulray in the short-lived series Bad Judge. He also starred as Russell in another short-lived show Truth Be Told. From 2017-2018, he played Carter in the comedy show Disjointed. He currently stars as Rick in the new sitcom Fam. His latest film role was in 2018’s Dog Days.
