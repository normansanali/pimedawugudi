Who Is Lawyer Yarelyn Mena? Wikipedia Bio

Yarelyn Mena is an associate attorney working at the Brown Rudnick LLP, but she is yet to have her own Wikipedia page. She has been working in a law firm since 2019.

She initially started her career in law by working as an Intern in Ader Investment Management LLC in 2013. After three months of internship, she worked as an Assistant Paralegal for Fishbach Hertan.

Yarelyn has got a degree in J.D degree from Fordham University School of Law in 2019. Similarly, she went to Hunter College and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts with a major in Psychology from the college in 2015.

She had also worked as a Summer Associate for Brown Rudnick LLP in 2018 for three months. She has experience working as a Legal intern in S&P Global Market Intelligence and Fluent, Inc for four months each.

Yarelyn Mena Is In Johnny Depp’s Legal Team

Yarelyn Mena is one of the lawyers present in Johnny Depp’s legal team as her law firm Brown Rudnick LLP is presenting the actor in the defamation trial which has been going on.

The legal team includes Ben Chew, Stephanie Calnan, Andrew Crawford, Rebecca MacDowell Lecaroz, Yarelyn Mena, Jessica Meyers, Samuel Moniz, and Camille Vasquez.

Amongst the legal team, Ben Chew and Camille Vasquez have played an evident role. Ben is a multifaceted litigator, and he has experience in representing clients in complex commercial litigations and arbitrations.

Chew had also previously represented Depp in two breaches of fiduciary duty cases in Los Angeles.
